Company Introduction

Hebei Wanjie Machinery Technology Co., Ltd. founded in 1999, is located in northern China printing machinery production base – Yutian, Hebei. It is one of the earliest enterprises specializing in developing label printing machines. The company has passed ISO9001 international quality management system certification and CE certification of EU, became the “High-tech enterprises in Hebei Province”, “Innovative Enterprises”, “Medium-small-sized enterprise of science and technology ”, won the title of “Municipal Award for Science and Technology Progress”, won the title of “Hebei Province Small and Medium-sized Enterprise Famous Brand Product”; wanjie technology center has been recognized as Provincial Enterprise Technology Center, wanjie also has seven Invention Patents, twenty Utility Model Patents, won the support of National Science and Technology Innovation Fund for Small and Medium Enterprises. We are responsible for the revision of five industry standards, such as “Adhesive Label Printing Machine Industry Standard”, “Roll to Roll Flat-bed Lightweight Die-cut machine Industry Standard”, “Printing Machine Terminology Industry Standard”, “ Printing machinery narrow-web rotary die-cutting machine industry standard”.
Superior quality and customer trust are our fundamental momentum to our success and development. We will make great efforts to develop, and advance together with our business partners all over the world.
Since our inception, we always adhere to the sustainable development strategy: "technology" as the guide, "market" as the base and “quality” as our survival platform. Now we have developed into a high-tech enterprise with strong strength of research and development. Our products are sold nationwide, as well as more than 30 countries and regions. Such as German, England, Italy, Spain, Portugal, America, Vietnam, Thailand, Russia, Iran, Malaysia, Indonesia, Turkey, Brazil, South Korea, Serbia, Australia, Netherlands, UAE, India, Ukraine, and Taiwan.
